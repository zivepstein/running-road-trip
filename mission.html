~48 States, 48 Runs~

The Running Road Trip project is an adventure around the continental United States that will be embarked on by four average runners with a passion of running. For roughly two months, we will weave through the continental United States. By running in every state, we hope to show the unifying effect running has, even though the locations and conditions for runs are unique.
