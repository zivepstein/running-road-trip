Our Mission

ADVENTURE / EXPLORATION / PASSION / PHILANTHROPY / FRIENDSHIP / CURIOSITY

The Running Road Trip is an adventure around the continental United States that will be embarked on by four average runners who competed in high school together in their hometown: beautiful Boulder, Colorado as they raise awareness and funds for Alzheimers.

→ A project/adventure/endeavor tied together by their common passion for running
